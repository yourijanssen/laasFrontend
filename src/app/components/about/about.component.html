About Project LaaS

Welcome to Learning as a Service (LaaS), a platform designed to help you borrow books and follow courses to enhance your learning experience.

Features

  • Borrow books from a curated library
  • Follow courses to improve your skills
  • Role-based access for Trainers and Trainees
  • Secure authentication using JWT

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless learning experience by combining book lending and course management into a single platform.
